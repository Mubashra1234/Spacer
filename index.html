Mission
A FinTech DAO for Wealth-Creation &
Sustainable Community Development.

The Moneyverse is a DAO operating a Money Service Business which
prevents poverty by providing the payment rails, applications, and
systems required for nations, organizations, & persons to register
assets, mint money, and build sustainable communities.

have equal access to economic prosperity!

enable individuals and organizations to harmoniously coexist with their cosmic

communities, and lost nations.
